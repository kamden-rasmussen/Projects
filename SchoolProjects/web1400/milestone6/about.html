About Us

Charlie & Henry, Inc. was established in 1948 and has been providing professional services to the Intermountain area for over 60 years. We are an employee owned company which brings to our client a sense of ownership in all our projects. Our team of Professional Engineers, Land Surveyors, and Land Planners have completed 1,000’s of projects.

These projects include but are not limited to the following:

  • Residential Developments
  • Land Planning
  • Commercial Centers
  • Industrial Complexes
  • Water Systems
  • Sanitary Sewer Systems
  • Storm Drain Systems
  • Erosion Control Plans
  • Roadway & Highway Design
  • Utility Corridors
  • Well Sites and Rights of Way
  • Capital Facility Planning
  • Flood Plain
  • Hydrology
  • Complete Land Surveying Services
  • Mining Claims & Surveys
  • Water Rights
