Charlie and Henry, Inc. provides a totally integrated package of Engineering, Planning, Surveying and Construction Engineering Management Services. Organized in 1948 C&H has provided services to the Intermountain West for over 60 years. C&H is dedicated to providing its clients with exceptional customer service together with the highest quality products and services. Regardless of the size of the project C&H has the resources and experience to complete it on time and on budget. Charlie and Henry has formed and been a member on a number of multi-disciplined teams which have planned, designed and managed large complex projects
